Overview

We are an established indie game development studio experienced in bringing ideas to life through innovative game design. We are primarily a small team of ambitious college students who wish to use their creativity to make a difference on our planet, as well as to create powerful and memorable gaming experiences.

Together, we created a relatively small community of enthusiastic modders, streamers, and content creators.

Game Design

At Nexus Realms, creative game design is the basis for our creative ambitions. By creating unique player experiences, we inspire future game design.

Our design projects feature unique ideas!
